## Academic Talks

### [Eric Budish: The High-Frequency Trading Arms Race](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0iomaB9u2L8)
**Chicago Booth, 2015**

The paper that changed how economists think about latency competition. Explains why speed races are wasteful and proposes frequent batch auctions.

**Key insight:** Competition for queue priority creates negative-sum game
